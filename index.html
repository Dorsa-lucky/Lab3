Time Management:

Time management is the process of organizing and planning how to divide your time between specific activities. Most people understand the importance of managing their time, but they can not follow their planning.
The first factor is to figure out where our time goes and then prioritize our tasks daily. There is different techniques and strategies for time management , however we only will explain the Pomodoro Technique.

Pomodora Technique:

  1. Decide on the task to be done.
  2. Set the pomodoro timer (typically for 25 minutes).
  3. Work on the task.
  4. End work when the timer rings and take a short break (typically 5–10 minutes).
  5. If you have fewer than three pomodoros, go back to Step 2 and repeat until you go through all three pomodoros.
  6. After three pomodoros are done, take the fourth pomodoro and then take a long break (traditionally 20 to 30 minutes). Once the long break is finished, return to step 2.

Required Tools:

  1. Mechanical timer
  2. pen
  3. pencil
